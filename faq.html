FAQ

Common questions

Short answers. Direct links to the rule set and step gates.

What does the grant pay for

The grant funds full capital for homeownership so the household holds title without mortgage debt.

What does the household pay after closing

Taxes, insurance, and maintenance.

What stops flipping or renting

A right of reversion or grant lien applies at closing. Selling or renting inside the restriction window triggers repayment to the General Pot.

Read the rules

What is the lifetime household limit

One grant per person or household for life. Adults from a funded household stay ineligible for life.

How do children from a funded home apply later

They apply as adults after one year verifiable income and 24 months clean history. No adult from the childhood funded home participates in the new application.

What are the step gates

Income, safety, ability, ethics. Each stage is pass fail.
