How it works

  1. Household applies, gets vetted through four step gates.
  2. Approved households receive a unique ID for directed funding.
  3. Donors fund a General Pot or a specific household ID.
  4. Closing includes deed restrictions and repayment triggers.

Non negotiables

One and done

One grant per person or household, per lifetime. Adults from a funded household stay ineligible for life.

Primary residence

A right of reversion or grant lien applies. Selling or renting inside the window triggers repayment to the General Pot.

Safety first

Background safety flags result in a permanent ban. The platform rejects unsafe placements.

Donors fund a pot or a person

Donors choose community support or directed support, with strong privacy defaults.

  • General Pot closes funding gaps and supports Level 3 emergencies.
  • Individual ID directs funds to a vetted household, using anonymous data stories.
  • Stripe Connect supports destination charges for clean routing.
