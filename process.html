Step gates

Vetting and safety

Four stages. Clear pass fail criteria.

Stage Logic Pass criteria Fail criteria
Stage 1, Income Stability first One year steady work, or six months with clean financial history Unverifiable income
Stage 2, Safety Hard ban Clear background Safety flags result in permanent ban
Stage 3, Ability Residual income Income covers tax, insurance, and 300 reserve Remaining adult income too low to maintain safe housing
Stage 4, Ethics Non worker audit Verified reason for non work, caregiver, student, disability Signs of exploitation or manipulation of the user or platform

What approval means

Unique ID assigned

Approved households receive an ID for directed donations.

Primary residence terms

Closing includes grant lien or reversion terms tied to occupancy.

Ongoing checks

Annual checks support enforcement during the restriction window.
