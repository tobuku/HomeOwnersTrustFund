Funding model

The Pot vs the Person

Two paths for giving. One goal. A primary residence owned outright.

Option 1, The General Pot

Purpose

Funds close the gap for households near the finish line, and support Emergency Level 3 requests.

Allocation

Allocation follows platform rules and household priority logic, with privacy preserved.

Option 2, The Individual ID

Unique household ID

Every vetted household receives an ID such as #HTF-7742 for directed funding.

Anonymous data stories

Donors browse stories with non identifying fields such as household size, county, and history duration.

Example data story

Anonymous story

Single parent, 2 kids

  • 24 month clean utility history
  • Burnet County
  • Verifiable income history meets Stage 1
  • Residual income meets Tax, Insurance, and reserve test

Story fields exclude names, address, employer, and SSN.

Payment routing

Stripe Connect supports destination charges so funds route to a household ID ledger or to the General Pot.
